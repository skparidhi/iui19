Designers easily get fixated over design solutions. Inorder to produce efficient concepts a design inspiration aid is much supportive. Dip 1.0 is an inspired version of Idea Inspire 4.0. where the main emphasis is given on making the user interface intelligent. Dip 1.0 was designed with much concentration over the knowledge representation after search. It has a fuzzy logic search with a backend framework of Fuse.js. CAD model representation and Augmented reality displays are some of the add-ons for knowledge represenation
